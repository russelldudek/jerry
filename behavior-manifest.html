Senior Product Manager, Agentic AI candidate

One customer promise. One governed behavior. Many deliberate surfaces.

Use this worksheet before a prompt or model change becomes a production behavior. It is intentionally decision-oriented: unknowns should remain visible rather than being disguised as maturity scores.

Release posture

SHIP

Coverage, authority, economics, monitoring, owner, and rollback are sufficient for broad release.

CANARY

Bounded exposure with explicit observation window, stop conditions, and rollback.

HOLD / HUMAN-FIRST

Required evidence or authority is missing; limits are stated and safe human ownership is preserved.
