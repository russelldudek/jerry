What I would not do

No rewrite-first strategy

Preserve working leverage and prove the behavioral abstraction before large-scale migration.

No single vanity metric

Automation rate is important, but not at the expense of false completion, customer trust, or hidden human work.

No capability theater

Voice and computer use become roadmap commitments only when a customer journey, authority model, evidence path, and economics are credible.

Day-90 leadership readout

A concise, decision-ready account of the current behavior system, the thin-slice result, customer and operational learnings, platform standards adopted, unresolved risks, relocation status, and the recommended next set of product bets.
