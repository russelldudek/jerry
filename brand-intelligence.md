# Jerry brand intelligence

Observed: 2026-07-14

## Source hierarchy

1. Jerry public website (`https://jerry.ai/`, `/about/`, `/careers/`, `/news/`).
2. Jerry public press-kit link, which was visible but not accessible through the connected Drive environment.
3. Jerry wordmark and app composition published in Plaid's official Jerry customer story. The unmodified partner-published composition is stored at `assets/company/jerry-plaid-customer-story.webp`. A generous canvas crop containing the complete, unaltered Jerry mark is stored at `assets/brand/jerry-wordmark.png`.
4. Jerry's Ashby careers image result was used as a visual cross-check for the pink wordmark.

## Asset provenance

- `assets/company/jerry-plaid-customer-story.webp`
  - Source: Plaid official customer story for Jerry.
  - Original URL is recorded in `sources.md`.
  - Treatment: locally stored; no recoloring, redrawing, or compositing.
- `assets/brand/jerry-wordmark.png`
  - Source: exact pixels from the partner-published composition above.
  - Treatment: canvas cropped with generous clear space; no part of the icon or wordmark is clipped, recolored, stretched, redrawn, or traced.

## Visual observations

- Jerry uses a vivid, approachable pink wordmark and call-to-action language.
- The current public site favors bold, highly compressed headlines, direct consumer language, white or near-white fields, black type, and energetic but simple product storytelling.
- The brand voice is confident, friendly, concrete, and lightly humorous; it avoids formal insurance-industry stiffness.
- Public product positioning emphasizes removing work from car and home ownership, proactive assistance, real licensed experts, speed, privacy, and one-app simplicity.

## Typography

No official font files were obtained. The campaign uses locally available geometric sans-serif fallbacks (`Avenir Next`, Avenir, `Century Gothic`, Futura, system UI) to preserve the observed bold, modern character without claiming official typography.

## Campaign visual metaphor

The visual system is a **behavior diff and compile surface**: six distributed sources expose drift and compile into one Agent Behavior Manifest. This is candidate-original, role-derived, and intentionally distinct from previous campaign metaphors.
